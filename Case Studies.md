# 📌 Case Study 1: Data Breach in E-commerce Company

## 🏢 Scenario

An Indian e-commerce company stores customer data (name, email, address, payment info).
A hacker exploits a vulnerability and leaks customer data online.

---

## 🚨 Problem

* Customer personal data exposed
* Possible violation of General Data Protection Regulation
* Company reputation damage

---

## 🛠️ GRC Actions

```text
Steps:
1. Identify affected data (PII)
2. Perform risk assessment
3. Notify authorities (within 72 hours - GDPR rule)
4. Inform affected users
5. Implement stronger controls
```

---

## 🔐 Controls Applied

* Encrypt sensitive data
* Implement access control
* Patch vulnerabilities

---

## 📊 Outcome

* Compliance maintained
* Legal penalties reduced
* Improved security posture

# 📌 Case Study 2: ISO 27001 Implementation in IT Company

## 🏢 Scenario

An IT services company wants international clients and needs **ISO certification**.

---

## 🎯 Goal

Achieve International Organization for Standardization ISO 27001 certification

---

## 🛠️ GRC Actions

```text
Steps:
1. Define ISMS scope
2. Identify assets (servers, data, apps)
3. Conduct risk assessment
4. Apply security controls
5. Create policies
6. Internal audit
7. External audit
```

---

## 📄 Documents Created

* Security policy
* Risk register
* Access control policy

---

## 📊 Outcome

* Company gets ISO 27001 certified
* Gains client trust
* Wins international projects

# 📌 Case Study 3: Payment System Security (PCI DSS)

## 🏢 Scenario

An online payment gateway processes credit card transactions.

---

## 🚨 Problem

Sensitive card data at risk

---

## 🎯 Requirement

Comply with Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard

---

## 🛠️ GRC Actions

```text
Steps:
1. Encrypt cardholder data
2. Restrict database access
3. Implement firewall
4. Monitor logs
5. Conduct regular vulnerability scans
```

---

## 🔐 Controls

* Tokenization
* Multi-factor authentication
* Network segmentation

---

## 📊 Outcome

* Secure payment processing
* Reduced fraud risk
* Compliance achieved

# 📌 Case Study 4: Insider Threat in Company

## 🏢 Scenario

An employee copies sensitive company data before leaving job.

---

## 🚨 Problem

* Data theft
* Intellectual property risk

---

## 🛠️ GRC Actions

```text
Steps:
1. Investigate logs (with SOC team)
2. Identify data access patterns
3. Revoke access immediately
4. Update access control policy
5. Conduct awareness training
```

---

## 🔐 Controls Applied

* Least privilege access
* User activity monitoring
* Data Loss Prevention (DLP)

---

## 📊 Outcome

* Insider threat controlled
* Policies improved
* Future risk reduced

---

## 🚨 Problem

## 🛠️ GRC Actions

# 📌 Case Study 6: Ransomware Attack

## 🏢 Scenario

Company systems are encrypted by ransomware.

---

## 🚨 Problem

* Business operations stopped
* Data inaccessible

---

## 🛠️ GRC Actions

```text
Steps:
1. Activate incident response plan
2. Isolate infected systems
3. Assess impact
4. Restore from backup
5. Report incident (if required)
```

---

## 🔐 Controls Applied

* Backup policy
* Endpoint protection
* Awareness training

---

## 📊 Outcome

* Systems recovered
* Improved security controls
