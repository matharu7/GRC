

# 🛡️ Compliance Frameworks – Short Notes (Easy Learning)

---

# 🇮🇳 Indian-Level / Commonly Used in India

---

## 🔹 1. International Organization for Standardization ISO/IEC 27001

### 📌 Purpose:

* Information Security Management System (ISMS)

### 🔑 Key Points:

* Risk management approach
* Security policies & controls
* Regular audits
* Continuous improvement

### 💡 Example:

👉 IT company secures client data using access control + risk assessment

---

## 🔹 2. Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard PCI DSS

### 📌 Purpose:

* Protect credit/debit card data

### 🔑 Key Points:

* Data encryption
* Access control
* Firewall security
* Regular scans

### 💡 Example:

👉 E-commerce site encrypts customer card details

---

## 🔹 3. National Institute of Standards and Technology NIST Framework

### 📌 Purpose:

* Cybersecurity risk management

### 🔑 Key Points:

* Identify
* Protect
* Detect
* Respond
* Recover

### 💡 Example:

👉 Bank uses IDS + incident response plan

---

## 🔹 4. Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX)

### 📌 Purpose:

* Financial data security & accuracy

### 🔑 Key Points:

* Internal controls
* Audit logs
* Accountability

### 💡 Example:

👉 Company maintains audit trail for financial reports

---

## 🔹 5. Federal Information Security Management Act (FISMA)

### 📌 Purpose:

* Secure US government systems

### 🔑 Key Points:

* Risk management
* Continuous monitoring
* NIST compliance

### 💡 Example:

👉 Indian vendor secures US government project

---

# 🌍 International-Level Frameworks

---

## 🔹 1. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

### 📌 Purpose:

* Protect user personal data (EU)

### 🔑 Key Points:

* User consent
* Data rights
* Breach report (72 hrs)

### 💡 Example:

👉 Website allows users to delete their data

---

## 🔹 2. Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)

### 📌 Purpose:

* Protect health data (US)

### 🔑 Key Points:

* Patient data privacy
* Secure records
* Breach notification

### 💡 Example:

👉 Hospital encrypts patient records

---

## 🔹 3. Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS – Global)

### 📌 Purpose:

* Secure payment systems

### 🔑 Key Points:

* Encryption
* Access control
* Monitoring

### 💡 Example:

👉 Global retailer secures payment systems

---

## 🔹 4. National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST CSF)

### 📌 Purpose:

* Global cybersecurity framework

### 🔑 Key Points:

* Identify → Protect → Detect → Respond → Recover

### 💡 Example:

👉 Bank improves security posture

---

## 🔹 5. Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX – Global)

### 📌 Purpose:

* Financial compliance

### 🔑 Key Points:

* Audit
* Controls
* Reporting

### 💡 Example:

👉 Company listed in US follows SOX rules

---

## 🔹 6. Federal Information Security Management Act (FISMA – Global)

### 📌 Purpose:

* Government data protection

### 🔑 Key Points:

* Risk management
* Monitoring
* Security controls

### 💡 Example:

👉 Cyber firm secures government systems

---

## 🔹 7. International Organization for Standardization ISO/IEC 27001 (Global)

### 📌 Purpose:

* Information security worldwide

### 🔑 Key Points:

* ISMS

* Certification

### 💡 Example:

👉 MNC secures global operations
